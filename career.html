ABOUT US


GRD Trucking was founded in 2017 by our President Harpal “Paul” Singh with just one truck owned and operated by himself.
His reputation in the industry and unwavering desire for customer service created what is now an award-winning Transportation Company.
GRD Trucking's on-time record is proven as we were awarded 2022 Carrier of the Year by Transfix with a 98% on time delivery rate.
